A safe way to help the homeless

EUGChange is a secure alternative to giving to panhandlers.

About

What?

Many of us are uncomfortable giving money to panhandlers, but really want to help people in need. EUGChange sends your gift to a dedicated fund at St. Vincent de Paul, providing direct service to homeless residents and helping you give wisely on the go.

Why?

Using EUGChange takes away the incentives driving dense concentrations of panhandlers in lively pedestrian areas such as downtown. It also provides an easy way to support experienced providers of services to people in need, document your real-time giving for tax purposes, and make sure your money will help improve lives.
